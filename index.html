About Me

I am a passionate .NET developer with a strong specialization in the airline industry, particularly in integrating Global Distribution Systems (GDS) and New Distribution Capability (NDC)(17.3,21.3) platforms. My journey began with a project that required real-time flight search, booking, and ticketing integration, and I found it fascinating how these technologies power the aviation sector.

Over the years, I have worked with leading providers like Sabre, Amadeus, Travelport, and direct airline APIs, including Emirates(NDC), OmanAir(NDC), Indigo(NDC), FlyHub, AirArabia, US-Bangla, NovoAir and Jazeera. I have also collaborated with over 15 aggregators worldwide, such as PKFare, MystiFly, Verteil(NDC), NuFlights(NDC), XMLDubai, Airpaz, Riya, LY, TraveloMatrix and AirIQ, in addition to working with GDS systems and airline APIs from regions including China, India, Dubai, and Indonesia. This experience has deepened my expertise in the domain, particularly in B2B OTA platforms.

Notable projects I have worked on include FirstTrip, TripLover, TravelChamp, TakeTrip, and TakeOff, where I contributed to creating seamless, efficient, and scalable systems for travel agencies and corporate clients. The complexity and impact of this industry continue to motivate me to specialize further, pushing the boundaries of innovation in the travel technology space.

In addition to my work in travel, I have also developed ERP systems for universities and corporations, providing comprehensive solutions for managing organizational processes. Moreover, I have hands-on experience in building VoIP calling systems, ensuring smooth and reliable communication for businesses and customers.
